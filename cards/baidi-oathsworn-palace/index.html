このカードの用語

  • レンジド（Ranged N）そのユニットがディスタントである限り、その攻撃は+Nパワーを得る。
  • シージアブル（Siegeable）攻城可能なドメインを表すカードサブタイプ。関連効果の条件・参照に使われる。
  • 耐久カウンター（durability counter）ウェポン等に置かれ、その耐久値を表すカウンター。攻撃や効果で減り、無くなると破壊される。

公式裁定（4件）

※裁定は英語原文です。

2025-03-02

Siegeable domains are not considered units. This means that being destroyed is not considered a death, and on kill and on death abilities won't trigger. Effects that target units cannot target Siegeable domains without additional typing.

2025-03-02

On hit abilities still trigger when dealing combat damage to a Siegeable domain.

2025-03-02

As of the release of HVN1E, the cleave keyword has been changed to attack all attackable objects rather than only units. This includes Siegeable domains.

2025-03-02

As of the release of HVN1E, the taunt keyword has been changed to block attack targeting from all attackable objects without taunt rather than only units. This includes Siegeable domains.
