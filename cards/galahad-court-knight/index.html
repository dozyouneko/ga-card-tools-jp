公式裁定（2件）

※裁定は英語原文です。

2022-12-27

Galahad attacking using a weapon is declared the same way a champion attacking with a weapon is.

2022-03-10

If Galahad uses a weapon, a durability counter on that weapon is removed as normal when damage is dealt. In addition, the weapon's effects are applied as normal.
