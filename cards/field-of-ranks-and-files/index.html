効果（日本語）

あなたの各ターン中に、チェスマン・アライが最初にあなたのコントロール下で場に出たとき、そのアライはターン終了時まで+2[パワー]を得る。

あなたの各ターン中にあなたが最初に起動するチェスマン・コマンドカードは、+2[パワー]を得た状態でインテントに入る。

効果（英語原文）

The first time a Chessman ally enters the field under your control during each of your turns, that ally gets +2 [POWER] until end of turn.

The first Chessman Command card you activate during each of your turns enters the intent with +2 [POWER].

公式裁定（3件）

※裁定は英語原文です。

2025-12-04

The first ability only triggers for the first instance of a Chessman entering each turn and will consider this retroactively. If a Chessmen ally already entered before Field of Rank and Files enters, it can no longer trigger in the same turn.

2025-12-04

The second ability only triggers for the first instance of a Chessmen Command card activation and will consider this retroactively in the same manner as the first ability. It will not apply to a second Chessmen Command card if the first did not enter the intent.

2025-11-30

The first time a Chessman ally enters the field under your control during each of your turns, that ally gets +2 power until end of turn. -> Whenever one or more Chessman allies enter the field under your control for the first time during each of your turns, choose one of them and that ally gets +2 power until end of turn.
